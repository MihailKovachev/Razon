>[!DEFINITION] Definition: Euclidean Topology
>
>The **Euclidean topology** on the [real vector space](../../../Algebra/Linear%20Algebra/Matrices/Row%20and%20Column%20Vectors/Real%20Vectors/Structure%20of%20the%20Real%20Vector%20Space.md) $\mathbb{R}^n$ is the [metric topology](../../../Topology/Metric%20Spaces/Metric%20Topology.md) induced on it by the [Euclidean metric](../../../Algebra/Linear%20Algebra/Vector%20Spaces/Inner%20Product%20Spaces/Euclidean%20Metric.md).
