>[!DEFINITION] Definition: Dense Subset
>
>Let $(X, \tau)$ is a [topological space](../Topological%20Spaces/Topological%20Space.md).
>
>A [subset](../../Set%20Theory/Subset.md) $S \subseteq X$ is **dense** iff its [closure](Closure.md) is $X$.
>
>$$
>\overline{S} = X
>$$
>
