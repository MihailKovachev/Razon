>[!DEFINITION] Definition: Metric Equivalence
>
>Let $X$ be a [set](../../Set%20Theory/Sets.md).
>
>Two [metrics](./index.md) on $X$ are **equivalent** iff they induce the same [metric topology](./index.md) on it.
>
