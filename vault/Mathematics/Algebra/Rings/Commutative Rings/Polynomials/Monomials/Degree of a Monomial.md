>[!DEFINITION] Definition: Degree of a Monomial
>
>The **degree** of a [monomial](Monomial.md) $M = a x_n^{p_n} x_{n-1}^{p_{n-1}}\cdots x_2^{p_2}x_1^{p_1}$ is $\max \{p_1, \ldots, p_n\}$.
>
>>[!NOTATION]-
>>
>>$$
>>\deg(M)
>>$$
>>
>
>>[!EXAMPLE]-
>>
>>$$
>>\deg(4x^4 y^3 z^5) = 5
>>$$
>>
>>$$
>>\deg(9 y^2 z) = 2
>>$$
>>
>>$$
>>\deg(xy) = 1
>>$$
>>
>
