>[!DEFINITION] Definition: Binary Operation
>
>A **binary operation** on a [set](../../../Set%20Theory/Sets.md) $S$ is an [operation](Operation.md) $f: S \times S \to S$.
>
>>[!NOTATION]-
>>
>>Instead of writing $f(a, b)$, we often write $a\, f\, b$.
>>
>
