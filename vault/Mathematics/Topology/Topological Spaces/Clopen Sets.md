# Clopen Sets

>[!DEFINITION] Definition: Clopen Set
>
>Let $(X, \tau)$ be a [topological space](./index.md).
>
>A [subset](../../Set%20Theory/index.md) of $X$ is a **clopen** iff it is both [open](Open%20Sets.md) and [closed](Closed%20Sets.md).
>
