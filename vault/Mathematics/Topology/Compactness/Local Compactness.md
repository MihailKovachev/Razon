>[!DEFINITION] Definition: Local Compactness
>
>A [topological space](../Topological%20Spaces.md) $(X, \tau)$ is **locally compact** iff each point $x \in X$ has a [compact](./index.md) [neighbourhood](../Topological%20Spaces.md).
>
