>[!DEFINITION] Definition: Molar Heat Capacity
>
>The **molar heat capacity** of a substance is the amount of [heat](../Heat.md) required to change the [temperature](../Temperature.md) of $1$ mole of that substance by $1$ Kelvin.
>
