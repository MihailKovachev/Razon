>[!DEFINITION] Definition: Commutative Ring
>
>A [ring](../index.md) $(R,+,\cdot)$ is **commutative** if its multiplication operation is commutative:
>
>$$
>a\cdot b = b \cdot a \qquad \forall a,b \in R
>$$
>
