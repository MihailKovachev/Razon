>[!DEFINITION] Definition: Total Order
>
>A **total order** of a [set](../../Set.md) $A$ is a [partial order](Partial%20Order.md) of $A$ in which any two elements are comparable:
>
>$$
>a \le b \text{ or } b \le a \qquad \forall a,b \in A
>$$
>
