>[!DEFINITION] Definition: Boundedness of Complex Sequences
>
>A [complex sequence](./index.md) $(a_n)_{n \in \mathcal{D}}$ is **bounded** iff there exists some $B \gt 0$ such that
>
>$$
>|a_n| \le B \qquad \forall n \in \mathcal{D}
>$$
>
