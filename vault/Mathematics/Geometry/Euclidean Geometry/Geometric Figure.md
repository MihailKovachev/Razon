>[!DEFINITION] Definition: Geometric Figure
>
>A **geometric figure** in the [Euclidean space](Euclidean%20Geometry/Euclidean%20Space/index.md) $\mathbb{R}^n$ is any [subset](../../Set%20Theory/index.md) $G \subset \mathbb{R}^n$.
