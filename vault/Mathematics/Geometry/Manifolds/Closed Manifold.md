>[!DEFINITION] Definition: Closed Manifold
>
>A [manifold](./index.md) is **closed** iff it is both [compact](../../Topology/Compactness/index.md) and without [boundary](../../Topology/Interior,%20Boundary,%20Exterior/Boundary.md).
